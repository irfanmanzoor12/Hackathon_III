# Bank Reconciliation

## Objective
Reconcile the cash/bank book with the bank statement for a specific period, identify discrepancies, post necessary adjustments, and prepare a reconciliation statement suitable for audit and management review.

---

## Prerequisites
- Bank statement for the reconciliation period
- Cash/Bank book or general ledger
- Prior month reconciled closing balance
- Access to QuickBooks
- List of issued cheques and deposits
- Authority to post adjusting entries

---

## Execution Steps

### Step 1: Verify Opening Balance
Confirm that the opening bank balance equals the prior monthâ€™s reconciled closing balance.

**Output**
- Opening balance confirmation  
- Discrepancy note if mismatch exists

---

### Step 2: Match Bank Statement with Cash/Bank Book
Compare each bank statement entry against the cash/bank book.

**Output**
- Matched transactions list  
- Unmatched transactions list

---

### Step 3: Identify Cheques Issued but Not Cleared
Identify cheques recorded in the books that have not yet cleared the bank.

**Output**
- Unpresented cheques list (cheque number, date, amount)

---

### Step 4: Identify Deposits Not Appearing in Bank
Identify deposits recorded in the books that have not yet been credited by the bank.

**Output**
- Deposits in transit list (date, amount)

---

### Step 5: Match Cheque Numbers and Amounts
Verify that cheque numbers and amounts appearing in the bank statement match the accounting records.

**Output**
- Cheque matching report  
- Discrepancy list (number or amount mismatch)

---

### Step 6: Identify Bank Charges and Interest
Review the bank statement for charges, fees, interest income, or adjustments not recorded in the books.

**Output**
- Bank charges and interest summary  
- Required adjustment entries

---

### Step 7: Post Missing Entries in QuickBooks
Post all missing or adjusting entries in QuickBooks, including bank charges, interest, or corrections.

**Output**
- QuickBooks adjustment log with reference numbers

---

### Step 8: Prepare Bank Reconciliation Statement
Prepare a formal bank reconciliation statement reconciling the book balance to the bank balance.

**Output**
- Signed-off bank reconciliation statement

---

### Step 9: Flag Unusual Items
Identify and flag unusual, high-value, repeated, or unexplained items for further review.

**Output**
- Unusual items report with explanation and recommended action

---

## Success Criteria
- Opening and closing balances reconciled
- All cheques and deposits properly identified
- Bank charges and interest recorded
- QuickBooks reflects reconciled balances
- Reconciliation statement prepared
- Unusual items clearly flagged

---

## Artifacts
- Opening balance verification note
- Unpresented cheques list
- Deposits in transit list
- Bank charges and interest report
- QuickBooks adjustment log
- Bank reconciliation statement
- Unusual items report

---

## Escalation Rules
- Escalate unresolved discrepancies
- Escalate unexplained transactions
- Escalate balance mismatches exceeding materiality thresholds

---

## Notes
This skill requires professional accounting judgment. It supports manual verification and posting workflows and does not assume automation.
