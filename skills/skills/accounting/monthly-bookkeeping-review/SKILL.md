# Monthly Bookkeeping Review

## Objective
Perform a complete monthly bookkeeping review to ensure accuracy, completeness, and compliance of accounting records prior to reporting, audit, or management review.

This skill represents **professional accounting judgment**, not automation.

---

## Prerequisites
- Access to vouchers and supporting documents
- Bank statements for the review period
- Prior month closing balances
- Access to QuickBooks
- Invoices and income records
- Authority to flag and escalate issues

---

## Execution Workflow

Follow steps **in order**. Do not skip steps.

---

### 1. Check Missing Vouchers
Review all recorded transactions and identify any missing or incomplete vouchers.

**Verify**
- Voucher exists
- Voucher amount matches entry
- Voucher date aligns with transaction date

**Output**
- Missing voucher list with transaction references

---

### 2. Review Expense Categorization
Check whether expenses are posted under the correct heads of accounts.

**Look for**
- Personal vs business misclassification
- Capital vs revenue errors
- Incorrect tax-related heads

**Output**
- Miscategorized expense report with suggested corrections

---

### 3. Identify Unusual Transactions
Flag transactions that are unusual based on:
- Amount
- Frequency
- Nature of expense
- Timing

**Output**
- Unusual transaction report with reasoning

---

### 4. Verify Opening and Closing Balances
Confirm:
- Opening balance matches prior month’s closing balance
- Closing balance reflects all verified transactions

**Output**
- Balance verification note or discrepancy report

---

### 5. Consolidate Issues for Review
Compile all issues identified so far.

**Output**
- Consolidated issue list with priority level (High / Medium / Low)

---

### 6. Verify Voucher Details
For each voucher, verify:
- Date
- Cash or cheque indicator
- Cheque number (if applicable)
- Receivable or payable nature
- Tax deduction or withholding
- Supporting document (cheque copy / invoice)

**Output**
- Voucher validation report

---

### 7. Reconcile Cash/Bank Book with Bank Statement
Ensure:
- All bank transactions are recorded
- Timing differences are identified
- No unexplained variances exist

**Output**
- Bank reconciliation summary

---

### 8. Verify QuickBooks Entries
Confirm that:
- All transactions are entered
- Entries are posted to correct accounts
- No duplicate or missing entries exist

**Output**
- QuickBooks verification log

---

### 9. Match Income with Invoices
For all receipts:
- Match payment to invoice
- Verify tax and deduction calculations
- Identify short or excess receipts

**Output**
- Payment–invoice matching report

---

### 10. Follow Up for Recoveries
Identify outstanding receivables and perform follow-up.

**Methods**
- Phone
- Email

**Output**
- Recovery follow-up log with status

---

## Success Criteria
- All vouchers accounted for
- Expense categories verified
- Balances reconciled
- Bank reconciliation complete
- QuickBooks entries validated
- Income properly matched
- Taxes and deductions verified
- Outstanding recoveries documented

---

## Escalation Rules
Escalate immediately if:
- Material balance discrepancies exist
- Missing vouchers exceed tolerance
- Unexplained unusual transactions persist
- Tax or compliance risks are identified

---

## Notes
This skill supports **human-in-the-loop accounting workflows**.  
It does not replace professional judgment and must be reviewed by a qualified accountant before finalization.
